Emergency Services & Trauma Care Unit

At Westin Hospital, our Emergency and Trauma Care wing is operational 24/7, providing rapid and efficient care for all emergency situations. Our team of skilled specialists, experienced nurses, and qualified emergency medical technicians are always on standby, ensuring immediate attention to patients in critical condition. The department is equipped with the latest medical infrastructure and supported by a round-the-clock diagnostic facility, guaranteeing the highest level of emergency care.

Our primary goal is to provide seamless, top-quality care, focusing on maximum patient comfort and clinical efficiency. We strive to integrate our team and patients into a smooth emergency response system, delivering care with integrity, professionalism, and precision at all times.

The Emergency and Trauma Care wing is supported by 24-hour laboratory and diagnostic services, enabling quick and accurate results. Our specialists communicate real-time instructions to emergency medical technicians via a wireless system, ensuring coordinated care during critical moments.

Additionally, we provide a dedicated emergency hotline that is available 24/7 to handle all emergency calls, ensuring immediate access to care whenever needed.
