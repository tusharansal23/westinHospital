Welcome to

Westin HOSPITALS

Westin Hospitals is a premier healthcare provider, offering a full range of advanced medical, surgical, and diagnostic services across multiple specialties. Our commitment to blending cutting-edge technology with compassionate care sets us apart as a hospital that prioritizes both excellence and the well-being of our patients.

A Unit of Albha Health Care Pvt Ltd

As a part of Albha Health Care Pvt Ltd, Westin Hospitals is driven by a mission to deliver specialized healthcare that is both accessible and of the highest quality. We are managed by a dedicated team of internationally trained and experienced healthcare professionals who ensure that every patient receives personalized, family-centered care.

Affordable Quality Healthcare

We believe that high-quality healthcare should be accessible to all, which is why we offer a range of services at affordable prices without compromising on quality. With a focus on patient satisfaction and well-being, Westin Hospitals strives to make world-class healthcare available to every individual in our community.

Emergency and Trauma Care at Westin Hospitals

Westin Hospitals offers 24/7 emergency and trauma care, equipped with advanced medical infrastructure and staffed by top specialists from various medical fields. Whether it's a life-threatening injury, sudden illness, or urgent medical condition, our team provides prompt and expert care.

State-of-the-Art Facilities & Expert Care

With the latest diagnostic tools and life-saving equipment, we ensure swift and effective treatment. Our specialists, including trauma surgeons, cardiologists, and neurologists, work together to deliver comprehensive, high-quality care in critical situations.
